"Building Bridges: Connecting NGOs to Empower Lives"

Our Aim

Our Aim is to develop an app to combat food waste & hunger by connecting NGOs with local establishments - restaurants/hotel. This platform enables food donors to upload surplus food details (type, quantity, expiry) for NGOs to view and arrange pickup/delivery. Incorporate a rating system for transparency and accountability. Plus give an option to restaurant customers to donate anything they want to from the restaurant menu and when restaurant has bulk of donation order they can make it and pass it on to NGOs.
